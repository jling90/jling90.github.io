About Me

Jan 13th, 2015 10:22 pm

Hi and welcome to my blog. My name is Jun. I’m currently a student at the University of Queensland studying a dual Bachelor of Engineering (Software) and Bachelor of Science (Genetics). I have a keen interest in programming, science and especially where these two worlds intersect. I spend a lot of my time in a linux terminal hacking together little scripts in the expectation that I’ll be saving myself time in the long run.

When I find the time I’m an avid reader. I’ll read anything I can get my hands on; science papers, tech blogs or fiction novels. I think there’s as much to learn from Kurt Vonnegut as Russel & Norvig.

I hope you’ll take the time to take a look through some of my stuff and ask uncomfortable questions about things I’ve gotten wrong or worded poorly. That seems to me the best way to learn.
